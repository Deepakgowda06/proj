About Medical Records

Keeping track of your medical records is essential for effective healthcare management. Our system allows you to:

  • Upload and store prescription details
  • Track medication schedules and dosages
  • Maintain a complete medical history
  • Access your records anytime, anywhere
  • Share important information with healthcare providers

All your medical data is stored securely and can be accessed by authorized medical professionals when needed.
